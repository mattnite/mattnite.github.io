CIRCUIT BASICS

CURRENT

  • Rate of flow of electric charge

  • 1 Ampere = 1 Coulomb/s
  • 1 Coulomb = charge of 6.241 * 10^18 electrons

VOLTAGE

  • Electric potential difference

OHM'S LAW

\[\begin{aligned} V & = IR \\ \end{aligned} \]
  • V: Voltage (Volts)
  • I: Current (Amperes)
  • R: Impedance (Î©)

KIRCHOFF'S CURRENT LAW

\[\begin{aligned} \sum_{k=1}^{n} i_k = 0 \\ \end{aligned} \]
  • Sum of current entering a node is equal to the current exiting that node
